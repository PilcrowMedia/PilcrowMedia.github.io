Pilcrow Media

Pilcrow Media is a digital media content production agency based in Vancouver, British Columbia. The organization specializes in branding, interface design, and content generation. The team is focused on cultural impact, and has led projects such as the Campaign for Culture, and the Campaign for Canadian Wine. Pilcrow Media is a spinoff venture from New District (newdistrict.ca) and currently resides in New District's Yaletown office. Wanna work with us? email us at go@pilcrow.media
